What to do if you solve P = NP

Let's say you just solved the P = NP problem – which is highly unlikely – what should you do next? Well it's even more unlikely to not know what you do assuming you just solved a major problem in computer science, so let's just imagine a time traveler or an alien found you and gave you detailed proof that P=NP along with an algorithm that can solve the SAT problem in polynomial time.

Evil path

  • Steal cryptocurrencies
  • Break encryption/passwords

Good path

  • Solve mathematical problems
  • Find bugs/bug bounties Entscheidungsproblem
  • Secure personal data
